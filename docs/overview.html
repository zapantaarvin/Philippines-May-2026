Group Weekend with friends
Style Relaxed, social, and polished

Notes

Weekend Feel

The plan is built to keep the weekend fun without feeling too rushed. Friday stays intentionally lighter, Saturday carries the main energy, and Sunday is meant to feel calm and easy.

Prep

What To Bring And Wear

Bring a valid ID, phone charger, power bank, cash or card, and comfortable shoes for moving around.

For clothing, keep it clean and presentable: casual outfits for daytime, and smarter night-out clothes for the evening.
